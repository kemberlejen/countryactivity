MOROCCAN ARTISTS

Saad Lamjarred

April 7, 1985 (40 years old)

Moroccan singer, songwriter, actor, multi-instrumentalist, dancer, and record producer. He is widely known for his music video "LM3ALLEM" which received over 1 billion views on YouTube.

Nadia Kounda

October 24, 1989 (35 years old)

Nadia Kounda was born in Casablanca, Morocco. She began her career in film and television in 2008, in Morocco. In 2011, she played the leading role in the film L'amante du rif, achieving recognition in her native country.

Dounia Batma

April 1, 1991 (34 years old)

Moroccan singer and actress who rose to popularity around the world as the runner-up of the first season of Arab Idol (the Arabic version of Pop Idol) on MBC.

Chaïbia Talal

1929 - April 2, 2004 (74 years old)

Chaibia Talal was a Moroccan painter born in Chtouka, a small village near El Jadida, Morocco in 1929. Chaibia Talal was married at the age of 13, before having a son, and become a widow at 15 years old. She was influenced by works of artists from the CoBrA painting movement.

French Montana

November 9, 1984 (40 year old)

Karim Kharbouch, better known by his stage name French Montana, is a Moroccan-American rapper. Born and raised in Morocco, he immigrated to New York City with his family at the age of 13 and began his career as a battle rapper in the early 2000s — under the name Young French.

Elgrande Toto

August 3, 1996 (28 years old)

Taha Fahssi, known by his stage name ElGrandeToto, EGT or Toto, is a Moroccan rapper, typically blending Darija, French, Spanish and English into his songs and performances.
